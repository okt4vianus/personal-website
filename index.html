About

Hey there! I'm a Remote Positioning Engineer with over a decade of experience in the oil and gas industry, currently making waves at Fugro Indonesia. Since 2014, I’ve been driving success as an AVTS Coordinator for TOTAL E&P Indonesia and as an AVTS Engineer for Pertamina Hulu Mahakam, focusing on Automatic Vessel Tracking Systems (AVTS) and optimizing maritime logistics.

I kicked off my career as a Software Engineer at TOTAL E&P Indonesia from 2010 to 2013, where I kept a close eye on transportation systems, managed personnel solutions, and built comprehensive marine crew databases. I also created insightful reports and led training sessions for users and technical support teams, boosting operational efficiency.

Before that, I was at Alita Process in partnership with Astrata as a Product and Software Engineer from 2006 to 2009. I managed tech teams to craft detailed mapping solutions and rolled out GPS tech for customer vehicles. My experience in quality assurance, user acceptance testing, and technical presentations laid a solid foundation in both software development and client relations.

I started my journey as a Network Engineer, tackling support and troubleshooting for computer systems and maintaining existing networks—this was where I really sharpened my technical skills.

Throughout my career, I’ve embraced the challenge of adapting to new technologies and industry shifts. I’m all about sharing knowledge and fostering collaboration, making sure best practices are a priority. I thrive in dynamic environments that push me to innovate and grow, always on the lookout for new tech to explore and apply.
